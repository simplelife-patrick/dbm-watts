******How to convert mW to dBm*****

The power conversion of mW to dBm is given by the formula: P(dBm) = 10 · log10( P(mW) )

So, 1mW = 0dBm

Example Convert 20mW to dBm:, P(dBm) = 10 · log10( 20mW ) = 13.0103dBm
